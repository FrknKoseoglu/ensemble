LITE PAPER ROADMAP CONTRACT

Ensemble is a project which will be developed by the coders in HeWO and will be improved by the participation of holders in Voting platform and thus by collective decision making.

Decisions relating to Ensemble will be made by the holders in Voting , which is one of the four pilars of Ensemble project.
Votes will be taken by holders via Smart Contracts
and participations in votings will be awarded by NFTs designed by digital artists, as a proof and reward of participation.

HeWo is one of the four pillars of Ensemble Project and decisions given will be implemented here. It is a platform where the coders can exhibit their projects of any kind and can be rewarded.

HeWo
A platform where the Ensemble Project is designed and developed with the holders.
VOTTO
Open-source software based and coder-awarded platform.
Barly
A platform where the NFTs given as reward to users or designed/purchased by users can be marketed.
KOBO
A DeFi project which will be built in BSC. Revenues obtained by KOBO will be used for the development of projects in HeWo.
